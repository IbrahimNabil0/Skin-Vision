About Project

Skin Vision is the fruitful result of our final project in our computer science degree. This initiative was motivated by the escalating demand for advanced tools to accurately diagnose skin conditions.Recognizing that precise diagnosis is fundamental for effective treatment, our goal was to create an advanced system capable of automatically identifying malria disease.

In order to achieve this, we used machine learning and image processing techniques. This led to the creation of an intelligent system that achieves an impressive accuracy rate on our dataset.

The dataset, comprised of 27,560 images, was divided into 80% for training, and 20% for testing. This division allowed us to refine our model to be robust and reliable, providing a high degree of confidence in its diagnostic capabilities.

Skin Vision is the successful end product of our computer science degree project. It epitomizes our dedication to learning new technologies, achieving our goals, and pursuing excellence. With the impressive accuracy of the system, we hope to help as many people as possible.
